# PakStyle BD – Pricing & Fee Structure

---

## Confirmed Rates (Locked)

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Logistics rate | BDT 1,600 / kg | Agreed with logistics partner |
| Platform commission | 10% of product BDT price | Applied after conversion |
| Transaction fee | BDT 100 flat | Per order, not per item |
| Conversion rate | Set internally by platform | **Not disclosed to buyers** |
| Minimum billable weight | 0.5 kg | Industry standard |

---

## Landed Price Formula

```
Landed Price (BDT) =
    [PKR Price × Platform Conversion Rate]     ← Product in BDT
  + [Estimated Weight (kg) × 1,600]            ← Logistics
  + [Product BDT Price × 10%]                  ← Commission
  + 100                                         ← Transaction fee
```

The buyer sees the total BDT figure. The PKR→BDT conversion rate is internal and not displayed.

---

## PKR → BDT Conversion Rate

- Platform sets its own rate (slightly above open market)
- Rate is **not shown to buyers** — they see BDT price only
- Updated weekly (every Monday)
- Locked at time of order confirmation

Example: if open market rate is 1 PKR = 0.38 BDT, platform may set 1 PKR = 0.42–0.44 BDT. The spread is platform revenue.

## Weight Estimation by Category

Used to calculate logistics cost at display time. Final billing is on actual shipped weight.

| Category | Est. Weight |
|----------|-------------|
| Lawn/Cotton Suit (unstitched, 3-pc) | 0.5 kg |
| Lawn/Cotton Suit (stitched/pret) | 0.6 kg |
| Formal Embroidered (chiffon/net) | 0.9 kg |
| Heavy Formal / Velvet / Bridal | 1.2 kg |
| Casual Shirt / Top | 0.4 kg |
| Dupatta (separate) | 0.3 kg |
| Accessories | 0.3 kg |

**Rule:** Always round up to next 0.1 kg. When in doubt, use the higher end.

## Multi-Item Order (Consolidation Benefit)

Logistics is billed on combined weight — significant saving for multi-item orders.

| Items | Combined Weight | Logistics Cost | vs. Separate Orders |
|-------|----------------|---------------|---------------------|
| 2 lawn suits | 1.0 kg | BDT 1,600 | Save BDT 1,600 |
| 3 lawn suits | 1.5 kg | BDT 2,400 | Save BDT 2,400 |
| 1 formal + 1 casual | 1.4 kg | BDT 2,240 | Save BDT 960 |

Consolidation is handled by the logistics partner at the Karachi warehouse.
