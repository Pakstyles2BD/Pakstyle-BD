## Sizing Notes

Pakistani sizing often differs from Bangladeshi conventions. Standard conversion guide:

| Pakistani Size | Approx. BD / International Equivalent |
|---------------|---------------------------------------|
| XS | 34 / UK 8 |
| S | 36 / UK 10 |
| M | 38 / UK 12 |
| L | 40 / UK 14 |
| XL | 42 / UK 16 |
| XXL | 44 / UK 18 |

Note: Unstitched suits are one-size (fabric sold by metre); sizing only applies to stitched/pret items.

## Brand Onboarding Checklist

For each brand added to the catalog:

- [ ] Verify website ships within Pakistan (to our consolidation address)
- [ ] Check ToS for resale restrictions
- [ ] Confirm accepted payment methods (Pakistani cards / international cards)
- [ ] Scrape or manually list top 20–30 SKUs
- [ ] Capture product images, descriptions, prices
- [ ] Map weight estimates by product category
- [ ] Test order placement end-to-end (place one test order)
