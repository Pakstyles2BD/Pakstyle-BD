### For a Non-Technical Founder | No Coding Required

---

## Honest Reality Check First

The custom tech spec (Next.js, Node.js, APIs) in `tech-spec.md` is **Phase 2** — hire a developer for that after you've proven the business works. For the next 3 weeks, you will build a **fully functional, real business** using no-code tools. Many successful e-commerce businesses run on exactly this setup permanently.

## ⚠️ Critical: Do These TODAY Before Reading Further

These two things take the longest and you must start them NOW:

### 1. Apply for bKash Merchant Account
bKash takes 2–4 weeks to approve. If you don't apply today, you won't have payments ready at launch.

**What you need:**
- Bangladeshi NID (National ID)
- TIN certificate (Tax Identification Number) — get from NBR website if you don't have one
- Trade license — get from your local City Corporation/Municipality (takes 3–7 days, costs BDT 2,000–5,000)
- Bank account in Bangladesh
- Business name (decide now: "PakStyle BD" or whatever you choose)

**How to apply:** Go to merchant.bkash.com or visit any bKash agent/office with the above documents.

### 2. Find Your Pakistan Consolidation Contact
You need a trusted person in Karachi or Lahore who will:
- Receive packages from Pakistani brands at their address
- Hold them until you say ship
- Hand off to your logistics partner (DHL/FedEx/TCS etc.)
- Send photos of everything received

**This can be:** A friend, family member, or a paid consolidation service (search "Pakistan parcel consolidation service"). Budget: PKR 500–1,500 per shipment handled, or a flat monthly fee.

## Day 3–4 — Logistics Partners

**Time needed: 1–2 days of calls/emails**

### Bangladesh Side (Last-Mile Delivery)
You need a courier that delivers across Bangladesh. Apply to at least 2:

| Company | Website | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| Pathao Courier | courier.pathao.com | Covers Dhaka well |
| Paperfly | paperfly.com.bd | Nationwide coverage |
| RedX | redx.com.bd | Good rates, tech-friendly |
| Sundarban Courier | sundarbancourier.com | Traditional, trusted |

Registration is free. You get a merchant account and can book pickups from your address.

**What to ask them:**
- Rate per kg for home delivery in Dhaka and outside Dhaka
- Cash on Delivery (COD) option (customers can pay cash on delivery)
- Integration with Shopify (Pathao and RedX have Shopify apps)

### Pakistan Side (International Shipping to Bangladesh)
You need a company to ship from your consolidation address in Pakistan to Bangladesh.

**Options:**
| Company | Notes |
|---------|-------|
| DHL Pakistan | Reliable, expensive (~PKR 1,200–2,000/kg), go to dhl.com/pk |
| FedEx Pakistan | Similar to DHL |
| TCS Pakistan | More affordable, tcs.com.pk |
| SkyNet Pakistan | Check skynet.com.pk |
| CargoToPK consolidators | Search Facebook groups for "cargo Bangladesh Pakistan" |

**What to negotiate:**
- Rate per kg (air freight, Pakistan→Bangladesh)
- Minimum weight
- Transit time
- Door-to-door or airport-to-airport
- Target rate: PKR 600–900/kg or BDT 650–800/kg equivalent

Get at least 2 quotes in writing (email/WhatsApp is fine for now).

## Day 10–11 — Add Products

**Time needed: 2–3 hours per brand, do 2–3 brands first**

### For each product, you need:
1. Product name
2. 2–4 photos (download from the brand's website — right-click save)
3. Description (copy from brand website, translate/simplify if needed)
4. PKR price
5. Category and size options

### How to add a product in Shopify:
Products → Add Product → Fill in:
- **Title:** e.g., "Gul Ahmed Summer Lawn 3-Piece Suit – GL-23001"
- **Description:** Include fabric, occasion, what's included (shirt + trouser + dupatta)
- **Price:** Enter the **BDT landed price** (your calculated total) — this is what customer pays
- **Compare at price:** Leave blank or use BDT equivalent of PKR price (to show "saving")
- **Images:** Upload 3–4 photos
- **Variants:** Add sizes if stitched (XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL)
- **Tags:** Brand name, category (Lawn, Formal, etc.)

### Show the price breakdown in the description:
In each product description, add a section like:

```
💰 PRICE BREAKDOWN
Brand Price (PKR):    PKR 3,200
BDT Equivalent:       BDT 1,344
Shipping & Handling:  BDT 525
──────────────────────
Total You Pay:        BDT 1,869

Exchange Rate: 1 PKR = 0.42 BDT (updated weekly)
```

This is your manual "landed price calculator" — transparent and builds trust.

### Start with these products (15–20 SKUs to begin):
- 5–6 products from Gul Ahmed (lawn)
- 5–6 products from Khaadi (lawn + pret)
- 3–4 products from Sapphire (casual)

## Day 13–14 — Payments Setup

**Time needed: 2–3 hours**

### Option A: Shopify Payments (NOT available in Bangladesh)
Shopify's built-in payment processor doesn't work in Bangladesh. You need:

### Option B: Manual Payment (for immediate launch)
While bKash/SSLCommerz approvals are pending, use this:

In Shopify: Settings → Payments → Manual Payments:
- Add "bKash / Nagad" as a manual payment method
- Instructions to customer: "Send payment to bKash: 01X-XXXXXXXX. Use your order number as reference."

This works immediately and is how many BD businesses start.

### Option C: When bKash Merchant is Approved

### Option D: When SSLCommerz is Approved
